No-Bake Chocolate Eclair Tart

Ingredients

  • 2 pkts of Tennis Biscuits / Digestives
  • 2 pkts Vanilla (or banana) instant pudding
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 400ml (Whipped stiff) Orley whip or cream

Ingredients for the Chocolate Topping:

  • 6 Tea-Spoons Cocoa
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of Icing Sugar
  • 3 Tea-Spoons butter (room tempreture)
  • 80ml warm milk
