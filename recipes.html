No Bake Chocolate Eclaire Tart




Ingredients

  • 2 pkts of Tennis Biscuits / Digestives
  • 2 pkts Vanilla (or banana) instant pudding
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 400ml (Whipped stiff) Orley whip or cream

Ingredients for Chocolate Topping:

  • 6 Tablespoons Cocoa
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of Icing Sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons butter (room tempreture)
  • 80ml warm milk


Mix Above ingredients till smooth. Add more Icing sugar if you like the frosting a little thicker. If it's too thick, just add a lillte more milk.


Method for tart

Mix Pudding and milk in a bowl. Blend together for about 2 minutes then fold in the whipped cream or Orley Whip.

Place a layer of whole Tennis Biscuits on the bottom, then top with 1/2 the pudding. Cover pudding with another layer of Tennis Biscuits, top with remaining pudding and another layer of Tennis Biscuits, then carefully spread the frostig over the biscuits.

Chill overnight in the Fridge.
