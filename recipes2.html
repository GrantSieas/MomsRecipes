Passion Fruit Cheesecake





Ingredients:

  • 150g Arnott's Marie crackers (18 Biscuits), or other sweet plain biscuits.
  • 100g melted unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar


Filling:

  • 500g Philadelphia cream cheese (softened)
  • 105 tablespoons plain flour
  • 1/3 cup + 2 tablespoons of Passionfruit pulp + 1/4 cup extra for swirling on top
  • 105 cups (330g) caster white sugar (superfine)
  • 2 tablespoons of Vanilla Extract
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 3 eggs




Directions for Passionfruit Cheesecake

  • 1. Grease and line 22cm/9" springform cake pan. Blitz biscuits into fine crumbs, then add butter and sugar and blitz until it looks like wet sand. Press into base of cake pan.

  • 2. Beat cream cheese for 30 sec or until smooth. Beat in flour. Add Vanilla, sugar and lemon, beat to incorporate. Beat in eggs one at a time until just combined. Stir in passionfruit. Pour into cake pan.

  • 3. Dollop extra passionfruit pulp across top, use knife to swirl. Bake 160^C/320^F oven (150^C fan) for 50 min- middle should still jiggle slightly. Cool in tin then refrigerate (covered) for at least 6 hours. Use paper overhang to lift out, cut and serve with dollop of cream or creme fraiche and more passionfruit!






Tips

  • No food processor? No probs! Place in ziplock bag and bash with rolling pin or something heavy.

  • Dont over beat, just beat to incorporate each ingredient.
