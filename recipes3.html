Chicken Chow Mein





Ingredients

  • 200g/6oz chicken thigh fillets, cut into small bite size pieces.

  • 1 & 1/2 tablespoons of peanut oil, 200g/6oz Chow Mein Noodles.**

  • 3 cups of cabbage finely shredded, 1 carrot julienned, 1 cup of bean sprouts.

  • 3 shallots/scallions cut into 5cm/2" pieces, 2 cloves of garlic fiely chopped, 1/4 water.


**Realityis, any noodles is fine here, even pasta! If using dry noodles/spaghetti, use about 120g/4oz then cook per packet instructions before using. Ig=f using fresh, use 200g/6oz per recipe.



Sauce

  • 1 & 1/2 tablespoon EACH Soy Sauce (normla or light, not dark)

  • 2 tablespoons of cornflower/cornstarch

  • Oyster Sauce

  • Chinese cooking wine (or Mirin or Dry sherry)

  • 2 tablespoons sugar

  • 1/2 teaspoon of sesame oil

  • White Pepper





Directions

  • Mix cornflour and soy sauce together, then mix in remaining Sauce ingredients.

  • Add 1 tablespoon of sauce to chicken, set aside for 10 minutes.

  • Prepare the noodles according to the packet instructions.

  • Heat oil in a wok or heavy basket skillet over high heat. Add garlic and cook for 10 sec. Add chicken and stir fry until the skin is white ~ 1 min.

  • Add the cabbage, carrot, and the white pieces of shallots/scallions. Stir fry for 1 & 1/2- 2 min.

  • Add the noodles, sauce and water. Stir fry for 1 min, tossing to coat the noodles in the sauce.

  • Add bean sprouts and remaining shallots/scallions. Stir through quickly then remove from heat. Serve immediately.
