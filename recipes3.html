Chicken Chow Mein




Ingredients:

  • 200g/6oz chicken thigh filltes, cut into small bite size pieces.
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons peanut oil, 200g/6oz chow mein noodles**
  • 3 cups cabbage finely shredded, 1 carrot julienned, 1 cup bean sprouts.
  • 3 shallot/ scallions cut into 5cm/2" pieces, 2 cloves garlic finely chopped, 1/4 water


Sauce:

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons EACH soy sauce (normal or light, not dark)
  • 2 tablespoon cornflower/cornstarch
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Chinese cooking wine (or Mirin or Dry Sherry)
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • White Pepper





Directions For Chicken Chow Mein

  • Mix cornflour and soy sauce together, then mix in remaining sauce ingredients.

  • Add 1 tablespoon of Sauce to chicken, set aside for 10 min.

  • Prepare the noodles according to the packet instructions

  • Heat oil in a work or heavy basked skillet over high heat. Add garlic and cook for 10 sec. Add Chicken and stir fry until the skin is white ~ 1 min.

  • Add the cabbage, carrot, and the white pieces of shallots/scallions. Stir fry for 1 1/2-2 min.

  • Add the noodles, sauce and water. Stir fry for 1 min, tossing to coat the noodles in the sauce.

  • Add bean sprouts and remaining shallots/scallions. Stir through quickly then remove from heat. Serve immediately.



** Reality in, any noodles is fine here, even pasta! If using Dry noodles (spagehetti), use about 120g/4oz then cook per packet before using. If using fresh, use 200g/6oz per recipe.
