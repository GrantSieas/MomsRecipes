Steak Mince Pasta Bake


Ingredients

  • 1kg of steak mince
  • 1 onion peeled and finely chopped
  • 5 cloves of garlic peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 chopped chilli
  • 1-2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of oil for frying onions
  • 2 cups of grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 pack of elbow or macaroni or shell pasta cooled per packet instructions




White Sauce Instructions

  • Step 1
    Fry your onions on medium to high heat until golden brown, next add your steak mince, black pepper, garlic, chilli and salt. Cook for 10-15 minutes (stirring when nedded).

  • Step 2
    Next add your tomato paste and sugar and cook for a few minutes. Add some hot water and cook on medium heat for 35 minutes until all water is cooked away and make sure you stir to preent mince from sticking to pot.

  • Step 3
    Next mix half of your white sauce with the mince mixture and add 1 cup of cheddar cheese and stir- pour this in a large baking tray or dish and pour over remaining white sauce and cheese.

  • Step 4
    Bake in a preheated oven on 200 degC for 30 minutes. Serve with green salad.
