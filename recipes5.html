Chocolate Eclaires




Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 120 grams of butter
  • 1 cup of Cake flour
  • 3 eggs
  • A pinch of salt




Directions For Chocolate Eclaires

  • Allow water, butter to boil in a saucepan.

  • Remove from heat, sift in the cake flour and mix with a wooden spoon.

  • Carry on mixing over a very low heat until paste forms a ball and the sides of the saucepan are clean.

  • Place ball in a mixing bowl and allow to cool for 10 minutes.

  • Once cool beat in the eggs one at a time, blending well after each addition. You may use a electric beater to beat the eggs into the dough.

  • Pipe or spoon mixture onto a greased baking tray or lined with baking paper and bake in a preheated oven at 200degC for 35 minutes or until golden brown.

  • Prick with a knife and allow to bake for a further 10 minutes on 160degC to allow the eclair to dry out.



Serving Options

  • Serve with Whipped Cream or Caramel

  • Serve with Whipped Cream and Strawberries

  • Serve with Whipped Cream and Bananas and Chocolate on top.
